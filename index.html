My Projects

Facebook

Facebook is a social networking platform that was founded by Mark Zuckerberg in 2004. It allows users to create a personal profile, share content, connect with friends and family, join groups, and follow pages of their interest. Facebook has grown to become one of the largest social networks in the world, with over 2.9 billion monthly active users as of 2021. It offers various features such as messaging, photo and video sharing, live streaming, events, and marketplace for buying and selling goods. Facebook also owns other popular social media platforms such as Instagram and WhatsApp.

Instagram

Instagram is a social media platform that was launched in 2010. It allows users to share photos and videos, apply filters and other effects, and share them on their profile or with their followers. Instagram has become a popular platform for visual storytelling, influencer marketing, and sharing personal and professional moments. In addition to posting content, users can interact with others by liking, commenting, and direct messaging. Instagram also offers features such as stories, reels, IGTV, and shopping that allow users to engage with content in different ways. As of 2021, Instagram has over 1 billion monthly active users.

Twitter

Twitter is a social networking platform that was founded in 2006. It allows users to post short messages, known as tweets, of up to 280 characters in length. Users can follow other accounts and see their tweets in their timeline, and engage with tweets by liking, retweeting, and replying to them. Twitter has become a popular platform for sharing news, opinions, and real-time updates on current events. It has also become a popular platform for businesses, politicians, and celebrities to engage with their followers and connect with their audience. As of 2021, Twitter has over 330 million monthly active users.
